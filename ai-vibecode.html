02 Process How I build with AI

Describe, iterate, ship.

01

Frame the intent

Start from a real campaign pain — what I want, who it’s for, and what “done” looks like. Plain language, no jargon.

02

Prompt & scaffold

Hand the intent to AI to scaffold a first version fast, then read every output critically instead of accepting it blindly.

03

Iterate in small loops

Tighten in short cycles — fix, test, refine — checking it actually works the way a campaign needs it to.

04

Ship & reuse

Ship the tool, then template it so the next campaign starts ahead instead of from zero.
